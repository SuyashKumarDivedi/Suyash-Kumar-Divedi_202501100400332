III. Travel Related Terms

Itinerary

A detailed plan of a trip, including destinations, activities, and timelines.

Backpacking

A style of budget travel where tourists carry all their belongings in a backpack, often exploring multiple places at low cost.

Excursion
A short trip or outing, usually for leisure, sightseeing, or an adventure activity.

Layover
A stop at an airport between connecting flights, usually lasting a few hours before continuing to the final destination.

All-inclusive
A travel package where accommodation, meals, drinks, and activities are all covered in one price.
